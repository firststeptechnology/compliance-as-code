## Overview

NIST Special Publication 800-171 Revision 2 establishes security requirements for protecting Controlled Unclassified Information in nonfederal systems and organizations.

The First Step Technology LLC NIST SP 800-171 Rev. 2 Compliance-as-Code Rule Library converts the complete requirement set into structured, machine-readable JSON.

The product is designed to support modern security engineering, compliance automation, AI-assisted review, and Governance, Risk & Compliance workflows.
